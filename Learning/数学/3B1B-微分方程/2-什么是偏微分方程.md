   ```ad-thm
title: 推导
设$T=T(x)$

$T(x_2)=T(x_1+dx),T(x_3)=T(x_2+dx)=T(x_1+2dx)$

一阶差分

$\Delta T(x_1)=T(x_1+dx)-T(x_1)=T'(x_{1})dx$

$\Delta T(x_2)=T(x_1+2dx)-T(x_1)=T'(x_{2})dx$

二阶差分

$$
\begin{align}
\Delta^{2} T(x_{1})&=\Delta T(x_{2})-\Delta T(x_1)\\
&=T(x_{3})+T(x_{1})-2T(x_{2})\\
\\
&=[T'(x_{1}+dx)-T'(x_{1})]dx\\
&=T''(x_{1})dx^2
\end{align}
$$
```
